Welcome to ITI Kathua

Government Industrial Training Institute Kathua (ITI) was established in the year 1981 by the Technical Education Department, Government of Jammu and Kashmir under the overall aegis of Directorate General Employment and Training (DGE&T), Ministry of labour and Employment, Government of India New Delhi.

This Institute is situated in the heart of kathua city near Moon light Cinplex, only the cinema in Kathua. Various other Govt. departments surrounds the Institute like District Treasury, Police Station, J&K Bank ltd, District TB Hospital etc. It is only 3 km south of busiest Pathankot Jammu National Highway 1A.

ITI Kathua is among the 53 Govt. ITIs presently existing in the State of Jammu and Kashmir. The ITI building has been inaugurated by then Honb'le Education Minister Jenab Ali Mohd Naik , on 10th August 1984 .
